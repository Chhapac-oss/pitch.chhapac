How we work
A system, not a slideshow

Six phases, one operating system. 80% of the early time isn't spent touching slides. It's spent making sure the right things end up on them.

  • Deep-dive conversations on your business, vision, and fundraising goals
  • Identifying investor expectations, positioning gaps, and audience psyche
  • Aligning on narrative direction, tonality, and key messaging pillars
  • Gathering all necessary data, insights, and materials from your side
  • Mapping the content structure: what goes in, what stays out
  • Building a slide-wise framework for flow, logic, and clarity
  • Crafting a sharp, persuasive storyline aligned with your brand voice
  • Iterative reviews with you to lock core messaging and positioning
  • Finalising a content flow that captures business and emotional hooks
  • A moodboard aligned with your brand ethos and pitch tone
  • Design direction for layout, palette, typography, and visual style
  • Aesthetic alignment locked before any design execution begins
  • Hands-on slide design with custom infographics and visual storytelling
  • Data visualisation that simplifies complex information
  • Iterative refinement for clarity, consistency, and investor-readiness
  • Final polish: alignment checks and brand tonality tuning
  • Investor-friendly formats: PDF, presenter, and editable master files
  • Consolidated revision rounds so the deck ships on the date committed

Timelines that hold: sprints close on the committed date, subject to timely consolidated review cycles. Feedback arrives as one list per round, and the engagement ends investment-ready.
