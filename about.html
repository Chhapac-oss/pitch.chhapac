About us

Founder-built, founder-to-founder.

Chhapac is a strategy and creative collective with a forte in narrative engineering for driven founders who are actively designing how we experience the future. Chhapac.decks is its pitch arm: we help founders turn complex businesses into decks investors understand, trust, and remember.

It is led by Chhaya Joshi, an ex-SaaS founder who scaled in Southeast Asia, so every engagement runs founder-to-founder, not vendor-to-client. The working belief is simple: you have too much information, and it can't fit in 10 minutes or 10 slides. Deciding what to use, and how it creates a hook: that's the work.
